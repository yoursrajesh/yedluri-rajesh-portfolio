About Me – Web Designer & Frontend Developer

I am a dedicated web designer who focuses on building clean, responsive, and user-friendly websites. My main expertise lies in frontend development where I convert design concepts into fully working websites using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. My goal is always to keep the structure simple while making the design visually attractive and responsive on every screen size. Over time I developed a strong interest in how websites are structured and how layouts behave across different devices. Instead of relying only on templates, I focus on understanding the fundamentals of layout design, spacing, responsiveness, and component structure. This approach allows me to build websites that are not only visually appealing but also organized and easy to maintain. One of my core strengths is building responsive designs. A modern website must work smoothly on mobile phones, tablets, laptops, and large desktop screens.
